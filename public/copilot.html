HR Service Center Copilot

Internal chatbot for HRSC colleagues to draft answers and complete requests faster

Use the chat widget (bottom-right) to help you draft responses, structure case notes, and work through process steps. Treat outputs as drafts and validate against the relevant SOPs, policy, and system data.

Internal use only. Do not paste sensitive personal data (e.g., medical details, bank details, ID numbers, full home address). If the case is high-risk or time-critical, escalate early.

Good uses

  • Draft an employee/manager reply in clear, neutral language
  • Convert messy notes into a structured case summary
  • Generate a step-by-step plan for a request (with checks)
  • Create a KCS-style knowledge article from a solved case

Escalate when

  • Legal / ER sensitivity (disciplinary, grievance, protected topics)
  • Payroll impact this cycle, retro changes, or termination pay issues
  • Policy ambiguity or precedent-setting decisions
  • Data privacy concern or suspected breach

Tip: Start your prompt with country + employee type + what the person asked + deadline/urgency + what you already checked in Workday/ServiceNow.
